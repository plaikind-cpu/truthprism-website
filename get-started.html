Get Started

TruthPrism works on any device with a browser. Here's everything you need to know.

Access & Pricing

TruthPrism gives every new user 5 free fact checks to try the app with no commitment. After your free checks are used, continued access requires a personal access code.

Access codes allow a set number of monthly checks and are available at a modest cost — there is no subscription, and unused checks carry over. To request an access code, contact us at paul@truthprism.app.

Using the Web App

TruthPrism works in any modern browser on any device — desktop, laptop, tablet, or phone.

1

Open TruthPrism

Go to truthprism.app in your browser, or tap the TruthPrism icon on your home screen if you've added it.

2

Enter your access code

On your first visit, enter your access code in the field provided and tap Save Code. Your code is remembered on your device so you only need to do this once. New users get 5 free checks before a code is required.

3

Paste an article URL or text

Copy the URL of any news article and paste it into the URL field, then tap Check This URL. Alternatively, copy and paste article text directly into the text field and tap Check This Text.

4

Read your analysis

TruthPrism returns a Factual Accuracy score, a Context score, a bias assessment, and a plain-language explanation. Analysis typically takes 10–20 seconds depending on article length.

Add to Your Home Screen

For the fastest access, add TruthPrism to your home screen so it opens like a native app.

📱 iPhone & iPad

  1. Open Safari and go to truthprism.app
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow pointing up)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add — TruthPrism now appears on your home screen

💻 Desktop (Chrome)

  1. Open Chrome and go to truthprism.app
  2. Click the install icon in the address bar (screen with down arrow)
  3. Click Install — TruthPrism opens as its own window
  4. Find it in your applications or taskbar going forward

Optional: iPhone Share Sheet

Set up TruthPrism in your iOS Share Sheet for one-tap fact-checking from any app. When reading an article, just tap Share → TruthPrism and the analysis starts automatically. Setup takes about 5 minutes.

Create the Shortcut

  1. Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone (built into iOS)
  2. Tap "+" in the top right to create a new shortcut
  3. Tap the shortcut name at the top and rename it "TruthPrism"
  4. Tap "Add Action", search for "URL", and select it
  5. In the URL field, type:
    https://truthlens-laikind.up.railway.app/app?url=
  6. After that URL, tap the variable icon and select "Shortcut Input" to append the shared article URL
  7. Tap "+" to add another action, search for "Open URLs", and select it
  8. Tap the ⓘ button in the bottom toolbar → turn on "Show in Share Sheet" → confirm URLs is checked under input types
  9. Tap Done

Using It

  1. Open any article in Safari or another app
  2. Tap the Share button
  3. Scroll and tap "TruthPrism"
  4. TruthPrism opens with the article URL ready
  5. Tap Check This URL to run the analysis

Important Note on AI Analysis

AI analysis may contain errors or omissions, and should be used as a starting point for further research — not as a definitive determination of truth. Always verify important claims through multiple independent sources before drawing conclusions or sharing information.
