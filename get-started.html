Using TruthPrism on iPhone

You can use TruthPrism directly from the iOS share sheet — no app download needed. Here's how to set it up:

  1. 1 Open Safari on your iPhone and navigate to truthprism.app
  2. 2 Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow pointing up) and choose "Add to Home Screen"
  3. 3 When reading an article in any app, tap Share → TruthPrism to analyze it instantly
