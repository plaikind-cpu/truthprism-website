Get Started

TruthPrism works on any device with a browser. Here's everything you need to know.

Using the Web App

  • 1

    Open TruthPrism

    Go to app.truthprism.app in your browser, or tap the TruthPrism icon on your home screen if you've added it.

  • 2

    Paste an article URL or text

    Copy the URL of any news article and paste it into the URL field, then tap Check This URL. Alternatively, copy and paste article text directly into the text field and tap Check This Text. For paywalled articles, pasting the text directly always works.

    ⚠️ Paywalled articles (NYT, WSJ, Washington Post, etc.) and sites requiring login cannot be retrieved by URL. Paste the article text directly instead.
  • 3

    Read your analysis

    TruthPrism returns a Factual Score and Context Score (each out of 10), along with a plain-language summary of its findings. Tap Click Here for Details to view a full in-depth report. Both can be saved for future reference. Analysis typically takes 10–20 seconds.

  • 4

    After your 2 free checks

    Once your free checks are used, you'll be prompted to enter a Prism Pack. Visit the pricing page to purchase. Enter your code in the app and tap Save Code — it's remembered on your device.

📲

Add to Your Home Screen

  • iOS

    📱 iPhone & iPad

    Open Safari → go to app.truthprism.app → tap the Share button (box with arrow pointing up) → tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add. TruthPrism now appears on your home screen. Must use Safari.

  • Mac

    💻 Desktop (Chrome)

    Open Chrome → go to app.truthprism.app → click the install icon in the address bar (screen with down arrow) → click Install. TruthPrism opens as its own window and appears in your applications.

Optional: iPhone Share Sheet

Set up TruthPrism in your iOS Share Sheet for one-tap fact-checking from any app. Setup takes about 5 minutes.

⚠️ Some articles behind paywalls may not be accessible via the share sheet — the URL will open in TruthPrism but the article content may not be retrievable. Copy and paste the article text directly as an alternative.

Create the Shortcut

  • 1

    Open the Shortcuts app

    Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone (built into iOS) and tap "+" in the top right to create a new shortcut.

  • 2

    Name it "TruthPrism"

    Tap the shortcut name at the top and rename it "TruthPrism".

  • 3

    Add a URL action

    Tap "Add Action", search for "URL", and select it. In the URL field, type: https://app.truthprism.app?url=

  • 4

    Append the shared URL

    After the URL you typed, tap the variable icon and select "Shortcut Input" to append the shared article URL.

  • 5

    Add Open URLs action

    Tap "+" to add another action, search for "Open URLs", and select it.

  • 6

    Enable Share Sheet

    Tap the ⓘ button in the bottom toolbar → turn on "Show in Share Sheet" → confirm URLs is checked under input types → tap Done.
