TruthPrism

Paste any news article URL or text and get a structured analysis of its factual accuracy and contextual completeness — backed by real-time Brave Search and a plain-language explanation of every finding.

  • Factual Score out of 10 — verifies key claims
  • Context Score — flags missing context & framing
  • Real-time Brave Search verification of every claim
