How TruthPrism Works

From raw article to verified verdict — in seconds. Here's what happens under the hood.

1

You share any article or claim

Paste a URL into the TruthPrism web app, use the Chrome extension's side panel while you browse, or share directly from your iPhone using the iOS share sheet. TruthPrism works with virtually any news article, blog post, or text you can copy.

2

AI reads and extracts the key claims

TruthPrism's AI (powered by Claude from Anthropic) reads the full article and identifies the specific factual claims being made — separating opinion from assertion, and flagging statements that can be fact-checked against known information.

3

Claims are analyzed for accuracy and bias

Each claim is evaluated for factual accuracy and the overall article is assessed for political or ideological lean. TruthPrism looks at language choice, source selection, framing, and omission — the subtle signals that reveal bias even in technically accurate reporting.

4

You get a clear, structured verdict

TruthPrism returns a confidence score (0–100), a bias rating, a summary of key findings, and a plain-language explanation of what's accurate, what's misleading, and what's missing. No jargon. No black box — just clarity.
