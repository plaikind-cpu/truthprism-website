About TruthPrism

Built for a world where every headline competes for your attention — and not all of them deserve it.

Why TruthPrism?

We live in an era of information overload. Every day, millions of articles compete for attention — and the incentives of modern media too often reward speed, outrage, and confirmation bias over accuracy and nuance.

TruthPrism was built on a simple premise: everyone deserves access to a fast, unbiased second opinion on what they're reading. Not a politically-motivated fact-checker with its own agenda — but an AI tool that applies consistent, transparent standards to every article it evaluates.

The name comes from the physics of light: a prism doesn't distort — it reveals. It separates what looks like a single beam into its true components. That's exactly what TruthPrism does with news.

How It's Built

TruthPrism is powered by Claude, Anthropic's state-of-the-art AI model, known for its exceptional reasoning, nuance, and commitment to honesty. Claude evaluates each article without a political agenda — applying the same standards to every source, every perspective.

TruthPrism is available as a web app on any device, and supports an iOS share sheet integration — so fact-checking is always one tap away, wherever you're reading.

TruthPrism was created by an independent developer passionate about media literacy and the belief that good information — and the tools to evaluate it — should be accessible to everyone.

A note on AI: TruthPrism is a tool to help you think — not to think for you. AI analysis is powerful but not infallible. We always encourage you to read critically, consult multiple sources, and form your own conclusions. TruthPrism gives you better information to make better judgments. The judgment is always yours.
