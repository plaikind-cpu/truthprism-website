Notes & Limits

  • TestFlight builds expire after 90 days. You'll get an Apple notification when it's time for an update — open TestFlight and tap "Update."
  • You can have up to 100 TestFlight beta testers per app. If you'd like to invite someone else, please ask first rather than forwarding the link.
  • Each app uses its own Pack — TruthPrism uses Prism Packs, News-Distiller uses Distiller Packs. Codes don't cross between the apps. Both Packs are free on request.
  • Apple may show a "beta" label or warnings — that's normal for TestFlight apps and doesn't indicate a problem.
⚠️ Some paywalled articles (NYT, WSJ, Washington Post) may not load via URL. For those, copy the article text and paste it into the app's text field directly.
