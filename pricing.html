Frequently Asked Questions

Do checks expire?

No — your checks never expire. Use them at whatever pace suits you, whether that's one article a day or ten in a week.

Does one code work across all platforms?

Yes — your Prism Code and its check balance works across the web app, iOS Share Sheet shortcut, and Chrome extension. They all draw from the same pool.

How quickly do I get my code after purchase?

Your Prism Code is emailed to you within seconds of completing payment. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within a minute or two.

What counts as one check?

Each article analysis uses one check — regardless of article length or how many claims are verified. Checks are only consumed on successful completion of the analysis.

Can I buy more when I run out?

Yes — just purchase another Prism Pack. You can use the same email address and the new code will start fresh. Checks don't roll over between packs.

What payment methods are accepted?

All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Payment is handled entirely by Stripe — we never see your card details.

Is there a refund policy?

If you have an issue with your purchase, contact support@truthprism.app and we'll make it right.
