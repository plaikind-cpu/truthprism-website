What News-Distiller Does

Instant Article Summaries

Paste any article URL or text and get a concise, well-structured summary in seconds — no more wading through lengthy pieces to find the key points.

Political Lean Analysis

News-Distiller assesses the political framing of articles — with a high bar for attribution. Center is the default; lean is only flagged when directly evidenced.

Executive + Full Report

Get a quick Executive Summary for a fast read, or toggle to the Full Report for in-depth coverage of every claim, source, and contextual detail.

Chrome Extension

Distill any article without leaving your browser. The News-Distiller Chrome extension adds a side panel — one click and your summary is ready.

iOS Share Sheet

Reading an article on your iPhone? Tap Share → News-Distiller. An iOS Shortcut sends the article straight to the app, analysis starts automatically.

Live Streaming Output

See the summary generate in real time — no waiting for the full response before you start reading. Results stream word-by-word as they're produced.
