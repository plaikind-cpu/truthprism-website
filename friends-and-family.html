Native iPhone & iPad versions of TruthPrism and News-Distiller exist, but they're currently distributed by direct invitation rather than the App Store.

📖About the iOS Apps

The Chrome extensions are the public, paid platform for both News-Distiller and TruthPrism. The iOS Safari versions exist for the same reasons most builders make iOS counterparts: many readers do most of their news reading on a phone, and a native Share Sheet integration is hard to beat for that use case.

For now, the iOS apps are available only by personal invitation. The same founding offer applies — first 250 users get free distillations and fact-checks. Whether they become publicly available on the App Store depends in part on how much interest there is. That's where the form below comes in.

TruthPrism for iOS

Fact-check articles directly from Safari's Share Sheet on iPhone or iPad. Same Factual Score, Context Score, and sourced verdicts as the Chrome extension.

News-Distiller for iOS

Distill articles to executive summaries and full reports from any app's Share Sheet on iPhone or iPad. Same political-lean analysis as the Chrome extension.
